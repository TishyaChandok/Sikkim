THIS PAGE IS ABOUT INFROMATION ON SIKKIM

Sikkim is one of the most popular tourist destinations in India and is famous for its natural beauty and Buddhism. There are numerous stunning lakes here such as Tsomgo Lake. Prayer Flags are quite famous here that are planted outside the homes of Buddhists for centuries.
